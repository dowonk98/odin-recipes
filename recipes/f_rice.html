Fried Rice

The best way to use up leftover rice! This is a very basic recipe for fried rice so feel free to spice it up with anything else available.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 2 eggs, whisked
  • 3 slices bacon, diced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, diced
  • 3 scallions, chopped, whites and green separated
  • 3 carrots, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas, defrosted
  • 4 cups cooked rice
  • 3 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil, more to taste

Directions:

  1. Heat canola oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add eggs and cook until curds are light and fluffly, approximately 2 to 3 minutes then remove from skillet. Add bacon to skillet and cook until crisp, approximately 5 to 6 minutes.
  2. Add pepper, scallion whites and carrots until soft, approximately 5 to 6 minutes. Add garlic and ginger until fragrant, approximately 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low and add peas and rice until warmed through, approximately 4 to 5 minutes. Fold in eggs and season with soy sauce and sesame oil.
  4. Garnish with scallion greens and sesame oil and serve.
